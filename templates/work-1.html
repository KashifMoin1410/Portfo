Winter Internship

January/2022 - April/2022


During the four-month internship, the main goals were to gain experience and learn new skills. Initially, I received training from HighRadius in various theories and tools that I would use in my final project. These included Java, SQL, JDBC, HTML, CSS, and Python, among others. Regular evaluation assignments were also given to monitor my progress. Additionally, the interactive nature of the training sessions made the experience more enjoyable. After the comprehensive training, I was given a final assignment that required full-stack development.
Our training equipped us with the necessary skills to execute every aspect of the project, including front-end and back-end development, web design, and machine learning. The objective was to create a B2B invoice management web application powered by AI.
As an aspiring computer science student, I had the fantastic opportunity to work with a provided dataset from HighRadius. My task was to develop a machine learning model that could help firms manage their accounts receivable and make predictions based on the available data. Overall, this experience allowed me to acquire fundamental knowledge and skills that will be invaluable in my future career.
