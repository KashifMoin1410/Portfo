Machine Learning Intern

June/2022 - May/2023


Started as a virtual internship in June 2022 and concluded as an in-office internship in May 2023. Nearly a year filled of experiences, knowledge, and adventures. Being a machine learning intern during this internship was a wonderful experience. I was able to acquire new abilities in addition to honing the ones I already had. The most often utilized tech stacks were Jira, Putty, Docker, Bounce, SQLYog, Anaconda IDE, and programming languages including Python and SQL. I had the chance to work on several projects including automation, machine learning, auto-templates, regex-based entity wrappers, and other topics. Some insights :
Automation - Successfully automated and improved the effectiveness of existing SQL queries as well as created new ones.
Regex-based Entity Wrapper - Achieved 35% automation through mapping identified entities from OCR extracts to correct keys using regular expressions.
Auto-Template - Increased the overall automation rate of A/R closing by learning user actions made on long tail documents.
Machine Learning - Studied and implemented new technologies to support machine learning applications.
Furthermore, we were frequently tasked with developing machine learning models based on different problem statements in evaluation tasks. I was a part of one of the best teams with leaders and teammates that were always encouraging. I was also designated as the team's POC.
I will always be grateful for this opportunity that provided me with such invaluable insights and helped me strengthen my skills.
